How to Play Pokemon Battle Academy Top Trumps

Objective

Battle against your opponent by strategically choosing Pokemon stats to win the most rounds! Score points by selecting the stat where your Pokemon outperforms your opponent's Pokemon.

Game Setup

  1. Click the start button on the game screen to begin.
  2. Two random Pokemon will be selected - one for you (Pokemon Trainer) and one for your opponent
  3. Your Pokemon card will flip to reveal its stats, while your opponent's card remains hidden

How to Play

Starting a Round
  • Your Pokemon card will be revealed, showing all its stats
  • Your opponent's Pokemon remains face-down (hidden)
  • You must choose which stat you think will beat your opponent's Pokemon
Making Your Move
  1. Study Your Pokemon's Stats: Look at your Pokemon's six stats:
    • ID: Pokemon's unique identification number
    • Speed: How fast the Pokemon moves
    • Attack: Physical attacking power
    • Defense: Ability to resist physical attacks
    • Height: Pokemon's height in decimeters
    • Weight: Pokemon's weight in hectograms
  2. Choose Your Best Stat: Click on the stat button you believe has the highest chance of beating your opponent
    • Choose strategically - pick your Pokemon's strongest stats
    • Remember: higher numbers usually win!
  3. Reveal and Compare: After selecting your stat:
    • Your opponent's Pokemon card will flip and reveal their stats
    • The chosen stats will be compared automatically
Scoring System
  • Win a Round: Your chosen stat is higher than your opponent's → You get 1 point
  • Lose a Round: Your opponent's stat is higher → Opponent gets 1 point
  • Draw: Both stats are equal → No points awarded
Continuing the Game
  • Click Next Round to play another round with new Pokemon
  • Your scores carry over between rounds
  • Keep playing to see who can achieve the highest score!

Strategic Thinking

  1. Know Your Pokemon: Recognize the Pokemon on your card and think about what they're known for
  2. Risk vs Reward: Sometimes a moderate stat that's likely to win is better than gambling on an extreme stat
  3. Learn from Previous Rounds: Notice patterns in the types of Pokemon that appear

Game Controls

  • Start Game: Begin a new game session
  • Next Round: Continue to the next round with new Pokemon
  • Stat Buttons: Click any stat button on your card to make your choice
  • Home Button Return to the main menu
  • Help Button: View these instructions anytime
