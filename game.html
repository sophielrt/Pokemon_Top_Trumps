How to Play

  1. Start Game - Two random Pokémon are selected for you and your opponent
  2. Your card flips to reveal your Pokémon's stats, opponent's stays hidden
  3. Choose a stat you think will beat your opponent's Pokémon
  4. Opponent's card reveals and stats are compared
  5. Higher number wins the round and gets 1 point!

Stats Explained

  • ID: Pokémon identification number
  • Speed: How fast the Pokémon moves
  • Attack: Physical attacking power
  • Defense: Ability to resist attacks
  • Height: Pokémon's height (in decimetres)
  • Weight: Pokémon's weight (in hectograms)

Strategy Tips

  • Pick your Pokémon's strongest-looking stats
  • Higher ID numbers often mean evolved, stronger Pokémon
  • Mix up your choices - don't always pick the same stat
  • Learn which stats different Pokémon types excel at

Scoring

  • Win a Round: Your chosen stat is higher than your opponent's → You get 1 point
  • Lose a Round: Your opponent's stat is higher → Opponent gets 1 point
  • Draw: Both stats are equal → No points awarded
